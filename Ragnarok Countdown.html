How to play

You're a thane climbing Yggdrasil. Answer one comprehension question per world. You have a per-question timer and three ravens (lives).

  • Right answer — climb a branch. Banked seconds carry over.
  • Wrong answer — lose a raven. Read the myth note. Keep going.
  • Timer runs out — also a lost raven.
  • Three lost ravens — Ragnarök. The wolf wins.
  • All nine right? — Asgard. You get a rank.
